Chess

An old game for modern gamers

...So What?

For those who are unaquainted with chess, it is a game where both players take turns moving their pieces. They can only move one piece every turn. The goal is to trap the enemy king and threaten him with capture.

Now, while this might be a stretch of the imagination, but this was one of the first popular turn-based strategy games which is on a grid. Games like Fire Emblem frequently take place on a grid-like space. And while the rules between these two games are very different, there is a strong possibility that this is the original game which gave inspiration for the grid board.

For those of you who are actually interested in the history of chess, you can go ahead and watch this video below from YouTube.
